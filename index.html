Our Goal

Our goal for this application is to provide an easy access to blood donors locating at different regions of the country and save the lives in need from any kind of mishaps or emergencies.


Services

The main purpose of this application is to connect the donors and patients directly in an interactive way.

You can either sign up as a donor or as a patient.

The patient portal gets access to all the available donors, you can search the donors by their blood group and then contact them directly for further details, or you can either register yourself as a donor by providing your required medical information.
